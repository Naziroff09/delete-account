Delete Account Request

You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data by filling out the form below. Your request will be processed within 7 business days.

⚠️ This action is permanent. Once deleted, your account and all data cannot be recovered.

📋 How to request account deletion:

  1. Fill in your registered phone number
  2. Select a reason for deletion
  3. Submit the form
  4. You will receive a confirmation via SMS
